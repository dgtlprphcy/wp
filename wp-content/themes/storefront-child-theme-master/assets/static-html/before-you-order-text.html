Before you order...

Just a few important things you need to know.

  • We only deliver to Leeds city centre – this map shows our delivery area.
  • If your company’s not registered, the minimum order value is £50 per day.
  • Login or register your company to get all the benefits.
  • To avoid waste, we don’t do same-day delivery. You can order for tomorrow or up to 10 working days in advance.
  • The cut-off time for orders is 10am the working day before you’d like delivery.
